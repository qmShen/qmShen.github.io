I am a research assistant professor at the Research Institute of Trustworthy Autonomous Systems, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). My major research interests include Data Visualization and Human-Computer Interaction. My research goal is to develop innovative Information Visualization techniques and Interactive Analytics systems, aimed at enhancing the exploration and analysis of big data. I have leveraged my expertise across various domains, including System Observability, Urban Informatics, Interpretable Machine Learning, and Augmented Analytics.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu. Prior to that, I earned my Master's and Bachelor's degrees at the College of Computer Science, Sichuan University in June 2012, under the supervision of Prof. Min Zhu.
